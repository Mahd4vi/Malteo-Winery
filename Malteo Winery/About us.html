Our History

The Malteo Winery, which was formerly tucked away among undulating hills, has stood as a tribute to dedication and skill for many years. Our vines are enveloped in the sun's golden rays as a symphony of flavours starts to play.

Our winery painstakingly tends to the best grapes, lovingly nurturing them until they reach their optimal ripeness. With roots firmly rooted in tradition. Our expert winemakers expertly combine art and science to produce wines that dance on the palate and leave a lasting impression.

We at Malteo Winery think that every bottle has a tale to tell. Each sip is a window into the commitment and affection put into our wines, from the sumptuous, velvety reds to the sharp, energising whites. Our dedication to sustainable practises guarantees that the land that sustains us now will flourish for subsequent generations.

As you enjoy the results of our labour, join us on a journey of sensory exploration. We cordially extend an invitation to you and your family to experience the enduring elegance and allure of Malteo Winery. Raise a glass to life's special moments while remembering that each bottle contains a history of quality.

Don't miss out on the opportunity to be part of our vibrant community of wine enthusiasts. Simply provide your name and email address to stay connected and explore the world of fine wines with us. Cheers to a journey filled with delightful flavors and unforgettable experiences!
